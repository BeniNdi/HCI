To be a member of HCI you are required the following:


1. you have to be actively active and willing to work as hard as possible for the company

2. You have to attend the meetings scheduled by leaders

3. You have to pay the entrance fee of 5,000Rwf (current amount for entrance it may change)

4. You have to consecrate your time for children and bring in something new for the company.
