Our History


Hear The Children International NGO, (HCIN) is an international non-government organisation. It was established in RWANDA- KIGALI by NIYONSABA Jean Pierre, in coordination with Prof YASETH and students from Ochio University, Mount Kenya University and University of Rwanda


Since 15 October 2017 after reading the catalog of “The Rights of Children as Stipulated by the UN’s Declaration 1989” HCIN is an organization that strengthens Children’s rights and provides aids support children in vulnerable place all over the world.We have partners in 10 Countries: RWANDA, UK, QATAR, USA, CANADA, ZIMBABWE, KENYA, UGANDA, TANZANIA and ETHIOPIA.

Our Mission

"To inspire international youth in the way the world treats children as a nucleus of every community".

Our Vision

A world in which individual child attains the right to live, education and involvement.

Our Values

1. Creativity: We are open to new ideas, embrace change, and take disciplined risk to develop sustainable solutions for and with children.

2.Collaboration:We respect and value each other.

3. Ambitions: We are demanding of ourselves and our partners, set high goals and are committed to improving the health standard of vulnerable children.
